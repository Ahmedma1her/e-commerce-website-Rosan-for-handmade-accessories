## 🧪 How to Test:

### Method 1: Browser DevTools

1. Open your site in Chrome/Edge/Firefox
2. Press `F12` or `Right-click → Inspect`
3. Click the device icon (📱) or press `Ctrl+Shift+M`
4. Test these devices:
   - iPhone SE (375px)
   - iPhone 12 Pro (390px)
   - iPhone 14 Pro Max (430px)
   - Samsung Galaxy S20 (360px)
   - iPad (768px)
   - iPad Pro (1024px)

### Method 2: Real Device Testing

1. Deploy to GitHub Pages/Netlify
2. Open on your actual phone
3. Test:
   - Navigation menu
   - Scrolling
   - Clicking links
   - Back to top button
   - All sections load properly
